About

Find Out More About Us

By consolidating available arable land through concessions with land owners, we hope to control a large portion of all available unused arable land in Uganda and beyond. After the concessions are made with the land owners, we would then cultivate crop on these lands based on the scientific research done on the particular parcel of land such as the soil tests, pivot sampling of the selected crop per acre before expansion would be carried out. Once we have a hold onto the largest available arable land, this gives us the positional advantage of controlling how much food that could be grown thus giving us an upper hand on the entire supply chain of food.

Choice lands & Modern technologies.

Our stretches of land are carefully picked and suitable to grow high demand food using modern technologies, contributing to the general food basket on the African continent.
